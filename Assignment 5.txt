1. Hadoop in layman's term:

    According to layman Hadoop is divided into a two component one is storage and another one is processing.

    The Storage is performed by the HDFS.

    The Processing is done by Mapreduce it uses hive pig etc.


2. Components of Hadoop framework

   Map Reduce
   YARN
   PIG
   HIVE
   HBase
